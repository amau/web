BIOGRAPHY

Amau received his design education from The Surrey Institute of Art and Design and The Central Saint Martin College of Art and Design. He worked for creative agencies, government departments, and major newspaper publishers in Hong Kong before he joined the HKIED as a teaching staff. His exhibitions in Shanghai Street Art Space "Neon multimedia installation" and "Ruins photography" were highlighted as part of Hong Kong collective memory.
